Research

My research is structured around two main axes:

Axis 1: Combinatorial optimization, graph theory, algorithms and complexity.

Structural properties of graphs.

Vertex and edge colorings (network security problems).

Vertex/edge-colored graphs (e.g., web graphs).

Graph operators and intersection graphs (clique, biclique).

Extremal graphs for Hamiltonian cycles and paths (routing problems).

Axis 2: Graph-based modeling, large-scale data analysis, and logistics optimization.

Large-scale data analysis and data mining (financial transactions, stock markets).

Anomaly detection (money laundering, market abuse).

Mathematical modeling of complex environments.

Optimization and operations research for logistics systems.
